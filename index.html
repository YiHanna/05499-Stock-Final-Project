Stock Data Visualization

Yihan Luo, Helen Zhao, Boyang Lin

As the gap between the rate of return on capital and the growth rate of output widens, people are more aware of the importance of investment, as it ensures present and future financial security. Our application aims to facilitate the investing decision and process. We aim to provide users with basic understandings of financial markets that will help them make decisions. We will present financial data in a useful, meaningful, beautiful and effortless way.
