Build Your Portfolio

Use the portfolio tool to test your strategy! Simply type in the STOCK TICKER (such as: AAPL, FB, GOOGL…) and the number of SHARES you want to add to your portfolio. We’ll show you what your portfolio looks like.

PIE chart is a good way to visualize the component parts of your stock portfolio.

Other than the traditional pie, we also provide you with a novel BUBBLE chart. It’s more fun and gives more information. Each stock is represented by a bubble where its x label tells you how much your share position has changed and its y label represents its percentage change in price. The size of the bubble is proportional to your current share position of the stock. Different colored bubbles represent different sectors. If you have a colorful bubble graph, be proud of yourself! Your portfolio probably has a high diversity score.
